## Qualitative Predictions

These are structural predictions that follow from the axioms:

1. **Dark matter is not a particle.** It consists of uncompressed multiway branches
   of the graph. No WIMP, no axion, no sterile neutrino. Direct detection experiments
   should see null results indefinitely.

2. **The cosmological constant is not fine-tuned.** Lambda = 3/N^2 is a natural
   consequence of the Margolus-Levitin bound on the toggle graph. There is no
   coincidence problem; Lambda decreases as N grows.

3. **w = -1 exactly.** The dark energy equation of state is exactly -1 (cosmological
   constant), not dynamical quintessence. This is because the toggle graph has
   no dynamics at the scale of Lambda.

4. **Proton is stable.** Despite R-parity violation, the proton is stabilized by
   Z3 triality (the generation quantum number). Baryon number violation occurs
   only through processes that change all three generation quantum numbers
   simultaneously, which is exponentially suppressed.
